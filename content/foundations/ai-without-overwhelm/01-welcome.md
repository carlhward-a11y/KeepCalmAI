# Welcome

You do not need to know technical language to start using AI.

This short journey will introduce AI in small, calm steps.

## What you will learn

You will learn what AI is, where it appears in everyday life, and how to try a first simple prompt.

## Why it matters

Many people feel that everyone else already understands AI.

That is not true. Most people are still learning.

## Try this

Take a moment and choose the statement that feels closest:

- I have never used AI.
- I have tried it once or twice.
- I use it sometimes but do not feel confident.
- I use it already and want clearer guidance.

## Key takeaway

You are not behind. You are starting from where you are.
