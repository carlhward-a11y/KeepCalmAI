# AI Without Overwhelm

This is the first reference journey for KeepCalmAI.

It is for beginners who want a calm introduction to AI.

## Purpose

Learners will understand what AI is, where they may already meet it, how to start a short conversation with an AI tool, and how to check responses.

## Audience

Primary personas:

- Margaret
- David
- Aisha

## Planned lessons

1. Welcome
2. What is AI?
3. AI is already around you
4. AI as a helper
5. Your first conversation
6. Writing your first prompt
7. Checking answers
8. Confidence check
9. Next steps
