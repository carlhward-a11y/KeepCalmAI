# Familiar technology

AI feels less strange when we connect it to tools people already know.

## Examples

Some tools can suggest words, routes, songs, products or photo groups.

The important idea is that the tool looks for patterns and offers a response.

## Try this

Think of one digital tool that gives you a suggestion.

Ask yourself: was the suggestion useful?

## Key takeaway

AI is easier to understand when it is connected to familiar technology.
