# AI as a helper

AI can help with thinking, writing, planning and checking ideas.

It works best when you treat it as a helpful tool, not as a final authority.

## One useful comparison

Think of AI as a calm assistant sitting beside you.

You can ask it for ideas, summaries or explanations, but you still decide what to use.

## See it

Suggested visual: a learner holding a notebook with a simple digital helper nearby.

## Try this

Ask an AI tool:

Give me three simple ideas for using AI in everyday life.

## Key takeaway

AI can support you, but it should not replace your judgement.
