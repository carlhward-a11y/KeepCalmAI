# Checkpoint

This is a pause point, not a test.

## Choose one option

- I am still unsure.
- I am starting to understand.
- I feel more confident.
- I could show someone else.

## What to do next

If you are unsure, repeat one earlier activity.

If you are more confident, try one new prompt of your own.

## Key takeaway

Confidence grows through small repeated steps.
