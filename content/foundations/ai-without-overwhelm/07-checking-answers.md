# Checking answers

AI can be useful, but it can also make mistakes.

It may sound confident even when the answer is incomplete or wrong.

## When to check

Check AI answers when the topic involves money, health, law, safety, work decisions or anything important.

## Simple checking habits

- Ask where the information came from.
- Compare the answer with a trusted source.
- Ask the AI what might be uncertain.
- Use your own judgement.
