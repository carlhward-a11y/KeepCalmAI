# What is AI?

AI stands for artificial intelligence.

In everyday terms, it means computer systems that can respond to questions, recognise patterns, generate text, summarise information or help with tasks.

## One simple way to think about it

AI is a tool that can help you think, write, plan and explore ideas.

It is not a person. It does not truly understand in the same way people do.
