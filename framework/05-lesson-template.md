# 05. Lesson Template

## Purpose

This template defines the standard structure for KeepCalmAI lessons.

Every lesson should feel calm, predictable and practical.

## Lesson metadata

Each lesson should include:

- Title
- Collection
- Journey
- Estimated time
- Difficulty
- Target persona
- AI platform relevance
- Learning objective
- Prerequisites
- Suggested visual
- Accessibility notes

## Standard lesson structure

### 1. Welcome

Set expectations and reduce anxiety.

### 2. What you will learn

State one clear learning outcome.

### 3. Why it matters

Explain the practical benefit in everyday language.

### 4. Learn

Introduce one idea clearly and briefly.

### 5. See

Show the idea using a visual, example, screenshot or short demonstration.

### 6. Try

Give the learner one practical action.

### 7. Reflect

Invite the learner to notice what happened.

### 8. Quick check

Use a short question or confidence checkpoint.

### 9. Key takeaway

Summarise the lesson in one or two short points.

### 10. Next step

Guide the learner to the next lesson, optional practice or support route.

## Step rules

Each step should:

- Teach one idea only
- Use short paragraphs
- Avoid unnecessary jargon
- Include one primary action
- Support screen reader and keyboard access
- Include alt text for meaningful visuals

## Lesson length

A full lesson should usually contain 3 to 6 steps.

If a lesson needs more than 6 steps, consider splitting it into two lessons.
