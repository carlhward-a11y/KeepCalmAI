# 03. Audience Personas

## Purpose

KeepCalmAI is designed for real people with different goals, confidence levels and experience. These personas guide content, examples and design decisions.

## Margaret

Age: 72

Uses: iPad and email

Goal: Use AI for everyday life.

Needs: Reassurance, simple language and clear step-by-step guidance.

## David

Age: 45

Uses: Windows laptop and Microsoft 365.

Goal: Save time at work.

Needs: Practical workplace examples and confidence.

## Aisha

Age: 20

Uses: ChatGPT on phone and laptop.

Goal: Study more effectively.

Needs: Better prompting skills and responsible AI use.

## Design rule

When writing any lesson ask: Which persona is this helping? Would they understand it without further explanation?
